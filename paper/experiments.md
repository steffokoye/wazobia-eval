# Experiments

## Pilot Evaluation

The initial pilot benchmark consisted of sixteen Nigerian Pidgin examples covering all emotion categories.

### Model Evaluated

GPT

### Results

Correct Predictions: 7

Total Examples: 16

Accuracy: 43.75%

---

## Observations

Performance was strongest on:

* Neutral
* Anger
* Joy

Performance was weakest on:

* Sarcasm
* Pride
* Market Energy
* Prayer Gratitude

---

## Interpretation

The results suggest that current frontier language models struggle with culturally grounded emotional distinctions in Nigerian Pidgin.

Further evaluation on larger benchmark sets is ongoing.
