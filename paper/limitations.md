# Limitations

Several limitations should be acknowledged.

First, the current benchmark remains relatively small and represents an early-stage evaluation effort.

Second, the pilot benchmark was evaluated on a limited number of examples and models.

Third, emotion annotation remains inherently subjective. Although inter-annotator review procedures have been introduced, additional annotator participation will improve reliability.

Fourth, contextual information is difficult to represent consistently within benchmark datasets.

Future versions of Wazobia Eval will expand dataset size, increase annotator participation, and introduce additional benchmark tracks.
