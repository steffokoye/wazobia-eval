# Conclusion

We introduced Wazobia Eval, a benchmark for evaluating Nigerian Pidgin emotion understanding, sarcasm detection, cultural reasoning, and contextual emotion disambiguation.

The benchmark contributes a culturally grounded emotion taxonomy, benchmark tasks, evaluation protocols, and pilot results demonstrating the challenges faced by current language models.

Our findings suggest that significant gaps remain between surface-level language understanding and culturally grounded reasoning.

By providing an open evaluation framework, Wazobia Eval aims to support future research, dataset development, and language technology for Nigerian Pidgin and other African languages.

Future work will expand benchmark coverage, refine the taxonomy, improve annotation quality, and evaluate a broader range of frontier language models.
