# Results

## Pilot Benchmark Evaluation

The initial pilot benchmark consisted of sixteen manually selected Nigerian Pidgin examples covering all emotion categories in the Wazobia taxonomy.

### GPT Results

| Metric              | Score  |
| ------------------- | ------ |
| Correct Predictions | 7      |
| Total Examples      | 16     |
| Accuracy            | 43.75% |

---

## Key Findings

The model demonstrated stronger performance on relatively universal emotional concepts such as anger, joy, and neutral statements.

Performance deteriorated substantially on culturally grounded categories including:

* Market Energy
* Prayer Gratitude
* Forming
* Pride
* Sarcasm

---

## Interpretation

The pilot results suggest that current language models often understand the literal meaning of Nigerian Pidgin expressions but struggle to infer their culturally grounded emotional intent.

This performance gap highlights the importance of culturally specific evaluation benchmarks.
